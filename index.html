Dispatched Business Studio is a modern take on an advisory firm.

We are a platform of trusted advisors working in collaboration with businesses, brands, and innovative founders. We provide curated business solutions that tap into a unique network of experts across a broad range of industries in the United States and internationally. Implementing our expertise and connections saves our clients time and resources, and allows them to focus on their core businesses and passions.

Our mission is to simplify and elevate the process of launching and expanding companies at all stages by delivering a comprehensive suite of services, expertise, and connections that empower entrepreneurs, innovators, brands, and investors to strategically accelerate.

Who We Are

We are creative former practicing attorneys with multicultural backgrounds and entrepreneurial mindsets who practiced law at major international firms and the world's largest asset manager. With over fifteen years of combined experience, we have structured, led, negotiated, and closed countless transactions amounting to billions of dollars in deal value for private and public companies across various industries. Our unparalleled insights, attention to detail, and extensive market knowledge were developed by navigating the demanding challenges faced by our clients while providing time-sensitive, savvy solutions.

Based in New York, with personal and professional experiences spanning the Americas, Europe, and Asia, we have a profound understanding and passion for all aspects of business, innovation, and design.

With our unique backgrounds and flair for simplification, we pivoted from providing legal advice to creating your dedicated business studio.

We also understand that entrepreneurs, creatives, and companies may not express themselves the same way as the external experts they require. By acting as an extension of your team, you leverage our expertise to bridge the gap between your vision and the resources to bring it to life.

  • Refine and curate your ideas, goals, and projects into actionable plans
  • Guide you through all business formalities from inception to expansion
  • Develop a customized roadmap to prioritize needs and ensure alignment with your overarching goals
  • Articulate initial challenges in simple terms and turn them into practical solutions
  • Save you time and resources with our sophisticated problem-solving approach

  • Evaluate your business objectives and leverage our industry expertise to create acceleration plans
  • Implement domestic and global market entry and expansion strategies, whether you are considering doing business in the United States, Latin America, or across the world
  • Navigate cross-border and cultural nuances in the increasingly interconnected business environment
  • Align strategy with execution to anticipate and mitigate potential risks

  • Collaborate with our platform of trusted industry leaders and facilitators to advise you at speed wherever you are throughout the lifecycle of your business projects
  • Initiate conversations and orchestrate your interactions with experts, such as legal, accounting, branding, marketing, manufacturing, importing/exporting, sourcing, real estate, luxury concierge, etc.
  • Act as the dedicated point of contact for you and your team to streamline communications, minimize moving parts, and reduce costs

  • Enable you to operate with ease and improve your competitive position to seize new opportunities
  • Break into new global markets with our cross-cultural insights of local specificities
  • Play an ongoing proactive role as you evolve and grow your business.

Our founders

Fabiola
Ordonez

Fabiola grew up in the border cities of Ciudad Juárez, Mexico and El Paso, Texas where she seamlessly connected across both countries with native fluency in Spanish and English. Her global expertise was further broadened by living in Hong Kong during her business studies. Prior to moving to New York, she spent three years in Shanghai where she co-founded an educational consulting firm and studied Mandarin.

In her legal career at Dentons US LLP and Kramer Levin Naftalis and Frankel LLP, Fabiola represented financial institutions, private equity firms, hedge funds, and asset managers, with a particular focus on structured finance and commercial real estate debt finance. Her most recent role was transactional in-house counsel at BlackRock, Inc. This experience representing top financial institutions provided first hand insight into the business realities driving complex transactions. Fabiola holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from Pepperdine University and a Juris Doctor degree from the Benjamin N.Cardozo School of Law.

Rodrigo
Castello
Branco

Rodrigo has over 10 years of experience in the intersection of legal and business globally. Originally from Rio de Janeiro in Brazil, Rodrigo is a native Portuguese speaker who has lived across the United States and in Italy, which led him to develop an international fluency and strengthen his diverse network.

He began his legal career at Baker McKenzie LLP in Rio de Janeiro and Miami, and later moved to New York City to practice at McDermott Will & Emery LLP. His practice focused on corporate matters, with an emphasis on cross-border and domestic mergers and acquisitions for public and private companies, including private equity and other investment funds, as well as joint ventures, venture capital investments, and restructurings.

Over the years, he has acted as the point of contact for companies across industries ranging from healthcare, biotech, fashion, real estate, technology, and energy. Recognizing the demand for forward-thinking advising on clients’ ambitions, he co-founded Dispatched. Rodrigo holds a Bachelor of Laws from IBMEC Law School and a Master of Laws from University of Pennsylvania Carey Law School.
